About Clean Juloos

Clean Juloos is a community-driven initiative focused on cleaning our roads and public spaces during Muharram and chehlum and we also distrbute food water and books for children from the name of "Sabil-e Sakina" during Muharram and chehlum. We believe that a cleaner environment fosters healthier lives and happier communities. Join us in this journey toward a greener, cleaner world.

Benefits of Cleaning Our Surroundings

Health Improvement

Clean surroundings reduce the risk of diseases caused by pollution and waste.

Environmental Protection

Maintaining clean spaces helps protect our natural ecosystems and wildlife.

Enhanced Aesthetics

A clean area looks more appealing and can uplift the mood of those living or passing through.

Community Building

Cleaning initiatives bring people together for a common cause, strengthening community bonds.
